Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

GoalChronicle Media ("we", "our", or "us") operates the GoalChronicle Media Facebook Page and associated social media accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our automated football news publishing service.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors or followers. Our service automatically publishes football news and match updates to Facebook and Instagram using the Meta Graph API. We do not store, sell, or share any user data.

How We Use the Meta API

We use the Meta Graph API solely to publish content (images and captions) to our own Facebook Page and Instagram account. We do not access, collect, or process any data belonging to users who interact with our posts.

Third-Party Services

  • Meta (Facebook / Instagram) – content publishing
  • Cloudinary – image hosting
  • Google Gemini – AI caption generation
  • Football API – match data

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We do not control their data practices.

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. Generated images are stored temporarily on Cloudinary for publishing purposes only.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact us via our Facebook Page: facebook.com/GoalchronicleMedia.
